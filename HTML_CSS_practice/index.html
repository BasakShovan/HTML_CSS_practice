ICC Women’s Cricket World Cup 2025  —  A New Era for Women’s Cricket ❤


The ICC Women’s Cricket World Cup 2025 marked a major milestone in women’s cricket history. Co-hosted by India and Sri Lanka from September 30 to November 2, 2025, the tournament featured eight teams competing in a round-robin and knockout format.

Beyond just the battle for the trophy, the event symbolized inspiration, competitiveness, and the growing power of women in sports. It showcased incredible talent, passionate crowds, and the rising global appeal of women’s cricket — truly a defining moment for the future of the game.
